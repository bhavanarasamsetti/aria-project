Fix & Test Summary:

Issue:
Firmware v2.2.0 caused a BMS-04 calibration mismatch, leading to incorrect battery readings, temperature spikes, and reduced charging efficiency across the fleet.

Key Fixes:
- Real-time fleet monitoring system with anomaly detection within 2 minutes
- BMS calibration validation before and during deployment
- Canary deployment strategy with staged rollout to prevent widespread impact
- Enhanced thermal management with predictive cooling and <1s response time
- Automated incident response with rapid rollback capability

Test Coverage:
A total of 25 test cases were designed covering:
- Real-time monitoring and anomaly detection
- BMS calibration validation
- Canary deployment safety checks
- Thermal management performance
- Automated rollback and recovery
- End-to-end system validation

Outcome:
The solution ensures early detection of issues within 2–5 minutes, prevents large-scale failures through staged deployment, and enables automatic rollback within 5 minutes to maintain fleet safety and reliability.
